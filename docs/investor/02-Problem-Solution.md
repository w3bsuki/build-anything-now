## Key Differentiators

### 1. **Verification System**
Every shelter is manually verified before joining. Donors know their money goes to real causes.

### 2. **Case-Based Donations**
Donate to specific animals, not just organizations. See exactly where your money goes.

### 3. **Impact Transparency**
Real-time updates: "Luna received surgery thanks to 47 donors including you!"

### 4. **Mobile-First**
Built for modern users. Donate in 3 taps. Apply to adopt from your couch.

### 5. **Multi-Language**
Supporting Bulgarian, English, German, Russian, Ukrainian from day one.
