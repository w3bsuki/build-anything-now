# Profile Capabilities Spec

- Owner: Product + OPUS
- Update cadence: Bi-weekly
- Last updated: 2026-02-06

## Account Model
One account per person. Role is not hard-gated at signup.

## Capability Domains
- Donor
- Finder / rescuer
- Volunteer
- Organization representative (clinic/shelter/store)
- Moderator/admin (staff assigned)

Users can hold multiple capabilities simultaneously.

## Onboarding Contract
- Signup remains simple and low-friction.
- Optional onboarding branch for organization claim.
- Detailed capabilities are configured in account/profile settings.

## Verification Layers
- Profile-level verification (identity/role signals).
- Case-level verification (`unverified` / `community` / `clinic`).
- Organization claim + approval workflow.

## Privacy and Safety Defaults
- Public location precision limited to city/neighborhood.
- Volunteer availability opt-in only.
- No public PII exposure in query responses.

## Required Account UX Surfaces
- Capabilities and participation settings.
- Linked organization claims status.
- Verification status and guidance.
- Mission initiatives hub entry.
