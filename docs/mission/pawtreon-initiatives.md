# Pawtreon Initiatives (Mission Roadmap)

- Owner: Founders + Partnerships
- Update cadence: Monthly (or after milestone changes)
- Last updated: 2026-02-06

## Positioning
Initiatives are Pawtreon platform-improvement missions, separate from individual rescue campaigns.

## Initiative 1: Drone Support Program (Roadmap)
### Purpose
- Faster detection of injured/lost animals.
- Support search in hard-to-access areas.

### Preconditions
- Stable trust and funding core in MVP.
- Legal/operational drone compliance framework.
- Trained operators and emergency protocols.

### Milestones
1. Feasibility and partner sponsorship
2. Pilot area definition
3. Prototype deployment and safety review
4. Scaled operations plan

## Initiative 2: Safehouse / Adoption Center (Roadmap)
### Purpose
- Close rescue loop after clinic care.
- Increase successful adoption outcomes.

### Preconditions
- Reliable clinic pipeline and adoption workflow.
- Partner shelter integration and operating budget.
- Governance, welfare standards, and staffing model.

### Milestones
1. Facility model and cost study
2. Sponsor/partner commitments
3. Pilot site setup
4. Intake-to-adoption operational launch

## Product Surfaces
- Home featured initiative module.
- Account/profile mission hub.
- Cross-link from `/presentation` and `/partner` narratives.

## Transparency Requirements
Each initiative must show:
- Funding goal and progress
- Milestone status
- Spend/use-of-funds summary
- Risks and next checkpoint date
